Privacy Parameters

Data compilation and platform operational privacy policies.

1. Core Account Encryption

Your account gateway parameters are protected via server-side session hashing protocols. SmartCart compiles minimum access parameters (`user_id`, `user_name`) purely to route catalog items and complete standard payment ledger receipts.

2. Session & Cart Allocation Tracking

Temporary active session cookies are leveraged strictly to track your active transactional allocations (e.g., your shopping cart snapshot arrays). These parameters expire automatically upon logging out of the User Console Terminal.

3. Document Generation Logging

Invoice PDF documents generated via client requests match data fields securely mapped directly to our internal isolated database instances. No analytical tracking models are shared with external edge marketing nodes.
